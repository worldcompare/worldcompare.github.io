Description

Free online tool where you can change between lower case and upper case letters, where you can capitalize, uncapitalize, convert to mix case and transform your text. It supports:

  • Lower Case
    The Upper case converter will let you transform all the letters of your text into uppercase letters. Simply copy the text that you need generating into upper case and paste the text into the box above and select the Upper Case button.
  • Lower Case
    The Lower case converter will let you transform all the letters of your text into lowercase letters. Simply copy the text that you need generating into lower case and paste the text into the box above and select the Lower Case button.
  • Capitalized Case
    The Capitalized case converter will let you converting the starting of every word of your text into upper case and leave the remaining letters as lower case ones. Simply copy the text that you want to generate into this format then paste the text into the box above and select the Capitalized Case button.
  • Inverted Case
    The Inverted case converter will let you converting the uppercase letters to lowercase letter and vice versa. Simply copy the text that you want to generate into this format then paste the text into the box above and select the Inverted Case button.
